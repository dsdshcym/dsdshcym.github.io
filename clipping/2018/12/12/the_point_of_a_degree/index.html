The Point of A Degree

The point of a degree is more of teaching you how to learn.

-- From 160: Praise Hands Emoji 🙌 (Vaidehi Joshi) - The Bike Shed

I'm really grateful that I somehow taught myself how to learn in my college.

Knowing how to learn a new technology/framework/language is one of the core skills of a developer. I also believe that the meaning of life is to explore the world around us and learn who we really are.1

I'm still in this journey of learning and hope to learn more stuffs.

Footnotes:

1

人活着的意义和人生的价值就在于提升心性、磨炼灵魂。
