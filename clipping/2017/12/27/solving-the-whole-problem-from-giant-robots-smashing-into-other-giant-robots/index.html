Solving the Whole Problem from Giant Robots Smashing Into Other Giant Robots

It's important to celebrate after finishing a task/sub-task, it's easier to tackle down the whole task if we get enough power in this celebration process.

  1. Break it down
  2. Finish the next thing
  3. Celebrate (Say "Yay!")
  4. Go on to the next thing
